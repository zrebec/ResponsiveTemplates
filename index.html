Quest 2: Running out of the water

What's the happen with water? It looks the water could be stolen from our water tanks!

This is a problem for people usually. You're live in small village and most people doesn't have access to the water pump from far city. They are reliant to their water tanks.

One villager found a horses hooves and small brown bag which probably clue (or band) lost.

The brown bag isn't empty too. You can find there photo a unspecified colored fox because photo is grayscale. There are also small embroidery with text "8528". Probably your band should find the right fox and show him this code. It could be clue or confederate.

If you find the right fox, ask him to how you can find the clue of your water?

But remember, foxes are sly and you never know it his told you the truth.
